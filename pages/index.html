Project Atomic:
Deploy and Manage Your Containers
in the Next Generation Container OS

Use immutable infrastructure to deploy and scale your containerized applications. Project Atomic mainly comprises Atomic Host, Atomic Workstation, and various container tooling.

Atomic Host

Atomic Host (AH) provides "immutable infrastructure" for deploying to hundreds or thousands of servers in your private or public cloud. Available in Fedora Atomic Host,CentOS Atomic Host, and Red Hat Atomic Host editions depending on your platform and support needs.

To balance the need between long-term stability and new features, we are providing different releases of Atomic Host for you to choose from.

Get Started

Atomic Workstation

Fedora Atomic Workstation (FAW) provides "immutable infrastructure" for your desktop experience.

Container Tools

Tools from our team help you create, deploy, manage, and secure containers.

Deploy using Kubernetes and CRI-O

Manage container hosts with Cockpit

Install, run, and scan with Atomic CLI
